Rhizomatic Labs is a minimally viable decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) that acts as both a virtual research foundation and a software development studio.

We aim to become a pioneering research and development foundation leading innovative projects in the decentralized systems, blockchain and cryptocurrency space.

Our goal is to do this by designing software and research solutions for the whole community to benefit from.

We will also support and invest in R&D projects that the DAO find both interesting and necessary for the space to move forwards and upwards.
